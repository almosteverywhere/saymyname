About Hit 'Em Up

The most memorable diss track of the East Coast/West Coast rivalry; 2Pac and the Outlaws really let the Notorious B.I.G. and his crew have it.

In this song, 2Pac and the Outlawz threaten to kill Biggie, Puff Daddy, Lil Cease, and Mobb Deep; call Lil' Kim ugly and Biggie fat; and say fuck you to just about everybody and even their mamas. 2Pac makes direct threats of violence and identifies the victims by name, he is not subtle in the least.

Pac took as an insult because it was released soon after he was robbed and shot in New York. Lil Cease, however, claims that Biggie recorded this track months before the Tupac Shakur shooting.

Tupac’s main reason for believing Bad Boy and his shooting to be connected was that Biggie and Puff Daddy were in the same New York record studio as Tupac when he was shot. Tupac claimed that Who Shot Ya? was confirmation that his assassin was sent by Bad Boy.

The Los Angeles Times released a story stating that Biggie and his men lead 2Pac to the studio where he would be later shot, but the story was based on some documents that were proven to have been forged! 2Pac was, however, still convinced despite an issue of apology from the LA Times to Puff Daddy for the claims made.

For more information on the Biggie/2Pac beef, be sure to check out the Documentary “Biggie and Tupac”, available for streaming on Netflix
