About California Love

“California Love” is a “ringtone rap” of the pre-ringtone days; Dre’s verse was written by a demographic-optimizer robot, and Pac tries to mindlessly play along

But fresh out of jail, he couldn’t help himself; Pac’s verse is perfect to the syllable. The only possible criticism is its incongruity to the surrounding song

Subsequent to recording this song, 2Pac and Dre had a falling out (2Pac thought Dre was a punk for refusing to testify at Snoop’s murder trial)

Samples Joe Cocker’s “Woman To Woman” for the tune and Street People’s “West Coast Poplock” for the hook (sung by Roger Troutman)
