About Against All Odds

Against All Odds is Tupac’s thought out diss towards his East Coast adversaries. It’s full of well thought out insults unlike Hit Em Up, which is basically Pac screaming how he’ll kill everyone.

PLUS IT’S THE REALEST SHIT HE EVER WROTE!
