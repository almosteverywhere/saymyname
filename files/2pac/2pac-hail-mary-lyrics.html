About Hail Mary

“Hail Mary” is in keeping with the Baroque themes of the Makeveli album, and also features some impressive verses by Pac’s little homies, the Outlawz (which is rare)

The song captures 2Pac zoning out the violence and negativity surrounding him, trying to have a convo with his Spiritual Mother
