About Changes

Changes is Prophet Pac’s timely meditation on race, class and politics

The line about black president made me shed a tear when I heard it right after Obama was elected (that was before he started to suck)

The emotional-ass beat is sampled from “That’s The Way It Is” by Bruce Hornsby and the Range.
