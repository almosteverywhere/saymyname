About Ain't Hard To Find

A song from Book 2 of 2pac’s album All Eyez on Me. It’s a track that features C-Bo and Richie Rich as well as B-Legit and E-40 who at the time were part of a rap group known as The Click. One of the song writers was also E-40’s brother D-Shot and even though he didn’t rap he’s still on the featured list by the song name on the CD.
