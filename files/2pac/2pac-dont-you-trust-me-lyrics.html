About Don't You Trust Me

Song is from 2004 album “Loyal to the Game”, which was produced by Eminem, and went #1 on Billboard 200
In the song, 2Pac telling a story about woman, who don’t trust him, and was always checking for him everytime and everywhere
Song was considered a highlight of the album, despite low quality mixed vocals
