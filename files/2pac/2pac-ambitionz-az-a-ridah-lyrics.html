About Ambitionz Az A Ridah

The first song on 2Pac’s 2 CD opus All Eyez On Me

Apparently this was the first song he recorded as soon as he was out of jail. This song acts as a prelude to Pac’s changing attitudes from being a political commentator to just a violent thug
