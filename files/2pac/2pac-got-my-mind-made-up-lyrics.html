About Got My Mind Made Up

Originally a Dogg Pound/Wu-Tang collabo, this song was mixed (with 2Pac eventually replacing Inspectah Deck’s verse) because it was “too lengthy” (wtf?!)

It’s for the best: although Inspectah Deck is an O.G., 2Pac’s verse shines with lyricism
