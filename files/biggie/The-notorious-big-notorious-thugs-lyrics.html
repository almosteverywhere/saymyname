About Notorious Thugs

A note on the Biggie/Pac beef:

For Pac’s collaboration with Bone Thugs — (the albeit still masterful) Thug Love — they had to slow down the beat for the duration of Pac’s verse

In this, the Notorious/Bone Thugs collabo, the beat doesn’t slow down!

Keep that in mind…
