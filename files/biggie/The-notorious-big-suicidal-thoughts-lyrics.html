About Suicidal Thoughts

In this final track off of Biggie’s classic debut album Ready to Die, Biggie calls up Puff Daddy in the middle of the night to explain to him that he’s about to commit suicide. He feels unloved because he’s been such a bad man his whole life, and the guilt is eating him up inside. In the end, he pulls the trigger and kills himself
