About One More Chance / Stay With Me (Remix)

“One More Chance” refers to girls begging the Notorious B.I.G. to sleep with them one more time after he stops calling. Ok, Biggie, very credible: “Please, enormous fat guy, lay your gut on me one more time before you move on to other pathetic, desperate girls!”

If you are able to suspend your disbelief though, this is one of the Most Sexual Rap Songs of All Time.

It features his one-time wife Faith Evans and uncredited vocals from Mary J. Blige.

It was the highest debuting single of all time tying Michael Jackson and Janet Jackson when it entered the Hot 100 at #5 at the time.
