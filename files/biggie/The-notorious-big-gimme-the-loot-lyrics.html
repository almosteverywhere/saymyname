About Gimme The Loot

Two men scheme to commit various robberies. The voices and characters are distinct — one is skittish and hyper with a high voice, and the other is more like Biggie’s familiar persona. It’s not immediately obvious that both are in fact voiced by the Notorious B.I.G.

The song samples include Something Old (“Coldblooded” by James Brown) and Something New (“Just to Get a Rep” by Gangstarr…actually I guess this song is pretty old now)
