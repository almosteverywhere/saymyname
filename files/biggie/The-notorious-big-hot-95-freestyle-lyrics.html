About Hot 95 Freestyle

The two GOAT MCs joined by Scoob (of Das Efx), Kane & Shyheim in a freestyle masterpiece

Contains lyrics from

  • Come On by Biggie
  • Nothing to Lose by 2Pac
  • On and On by Shyheim
  • The Way it’s Goin' Down and Show and Prove by Big Daddy Kane

Additionally, the word freestyle is a bit of a misnomer. In the early to mid 90’s when this was recorded, a freestyle was commonly regarded as anything not recorded- not necessarily something off the top. This was not off the top- listen to Big Daddy Kane in the background of some of the verses providing layering- something that would be impossible had the artist been spitting impromptu.
