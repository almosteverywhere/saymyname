About Juicy

The first single from Ready to Die, this is the track that made Notorious B.I.G. a legend

Juicy was one of the first mainstream rap songs to discuss the rapper’s rise to the top, but it’s also the story of how hip-hop itself became popular.

The sample and title are lifted from Mtume’s classic, “Juicy Fruit”
