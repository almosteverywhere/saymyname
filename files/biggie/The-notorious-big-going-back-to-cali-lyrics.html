About Going back to Cali

4th Track off of the 2nd disc of Life After Death. The song contains a sample of “More Bounce To The Ounce” by Zapp.

Biggie addresses that despite rivalries East Coast–West Coast rivalries, he has nothing against California or the west coast and that he only has beef with people that have beef with him. He loves New York, but in his mind there is nothing wrong with a visit out west.

Ironically enough, the city Biggie loved visiting would be the last city he was alive in.

This joint goes off of LL Cool J’s 1987 song “Going Back to Cali”. Perhaps Biggie was influenced by LL Cool J in the late 80’s while he was growing up in Brooklyn???
