About Blow the Whistle

This song was played at one of Jay-Z’s clubs as a result of an NBA rivalry primarily between former 2008 Washington Wizards' SG Deshawn Stevenson and former Cleveland Cavaliers' Lebron James. This rivalry heated up in the first round of the playoffs (in 2008 as previously stated) because LeBron stated that Stevenson’s comments about him was like Soulja Boy talking smack about Jay-Z. Jay-Z, being acquaintances with LeBron decided to make a song dissing Stevenson (Maybe hoping that LeBron would go to New Jersey come 2011?)
