About Holy Grail

This song is about Jay Z’s and Justin Timberlake love/hate affair for fame. Fame can bring your spirits up and knock you right back down.

As part of the #newrules,

https://twitter.com/S_C_/status/346682205168357377

Samsung users were able to download the Magna Carta app and preview the lyrics for “Holy Grail” which features forthcoming Legends Of The Summer tour mate Justin Timberlake. Also, we get a glimpse of the Nirvana lyrics that Courtney Love signed off on in the chorus, for MCHG.

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B--ZARCwSIE

Contains a sample of Smells LIke Teen Spirit.

In the interview below, Jay confirms that the song is about fame.

http://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_detailpage&v=mo6GzsBi9wU#t=1461s

Here’s Jay breaking the track down with Zane Lowe on a UK radio show.

http://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_embedded&v=2XKRykuZH-I
