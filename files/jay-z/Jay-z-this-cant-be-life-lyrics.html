About This Can't Be Life

Jay-Z, Beanie Sigel, and Scarface are all very sad and wonder if there’s any point to living in pain. Maybe they recently had a book group discussion of Hamlet?

The beat is a hodge-podge, sampling Harold Melvin and the Bluenote’s “Miss You” (which was also recently sampled by Big Boi in “Shine Blockas”)

The drums are sampled from Dr. Dre’s “Xxplosive”

Kanye West discusses the process of creating and pitching this beat in his song “Last Call”
