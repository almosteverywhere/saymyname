About Ain't No Nigga

The second single off Reasonable Doubt. The clean version is “Ain’t No Playa”.

The song got Jay-Z his earliest taste of national recognition when Irv Gotti and Lyor Cohen got it used in The Nutty Professor.

Foxy Brown was just 16 years old at the time.

This is also the song that caught Def Jam’s attention and led them invest in Roc-A-Fella.

Jay-Z — not much of a weed user — reportedly got stoned just before they began filming the video, which features a cameo from the Notorious B.I.G. and opens with a scene similar to a scene from Scarface:
