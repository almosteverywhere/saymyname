About On To The Next One

A Swizz Beats produced beat from Jigga’s The Blueprint 3; Swizz also came up with the words to the hook. It samples a very obscure part from the song D.A.N.C.E. by Justice

The beat has become a popular freestyling platform (good luck getting it out of your head!)
