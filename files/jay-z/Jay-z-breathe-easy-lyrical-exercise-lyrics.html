About Breathe Easy (Lyrical Exercise)

This “hidden” song from Jay-Z’s classic 2001 album The Blueprint sees Jay using an extended exercise metaphor for his rap skills

The dark, piano driven beat was produced by Just Blaze and features a sample from Stanley Clarke’s “Got to Find My Own Place”
