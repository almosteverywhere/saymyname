About D'Evils

A meditation about how the desire for money and power corrupts the ghetto and all of its inhabitants, leading to violence and betrayal. The title is pronounced as “da evils”, but spelt like “devils”, emphasizing how the temptation of money is similar to the constant temptations of Satan.

DJ Premier samples “Go Back Home” by Allen Toussaint on the beat. Jay says this is possibly his favorite song, and he likes how they sampled a gospel record on a song about devils and temptation, saying

“I guess it’s fitting that it’s a gospel record, because it’s almost a religious experience
