About Girls, Girls, Girls

Jay takes what some would consider a racist and offensive view of the world’s females. Others find the lyrics quite clever. Regardless, the song contains a sample of “There’s Nothing In This World That Can Stop Me From Loving You” by Tom Brock.

Just Blaze made this beat with Ghostface Killah in mind, but Ghost turned it down.

Jay has stated that the song features uncredited background vocals from Michael Jackson.
