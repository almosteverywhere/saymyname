About I Know What Girls Like

This is the third track on Jay-Z’s second album Reasonable Doubt. This song a is contemporary and inner city take on the song “I Know What Boys Like” by The Waitresses. Jay-Z shares how he entices the women with his “jewels” and luxurious cars. In a sharp contrast, Lil Kim flaunts her ample assets: her “booty like POW!” and her ability to keep it “real.” Both genders represent ways to be attractive to the other sex. A young,hungry Puff Daddy helped produce this track and sing in the chorus. When this album came out in 1997, Diddy was one of the hottest artists in the game, famous for his production skills and hit making abilities. His style of creating beats featuring plenty of rock, pop, and rap samples has help make him rich and successful. “I Know What Girls Like” is a fine example of his production style. This song samples “A Fly Girl” by Boogie Boys.
