About Don't You Know

From the Paid in Full Soundtrack.

Per the track’s producer, Just Blaze:

Originally, that was a Nas diss record. … Then, we changed it around. There were still subtle shots at Nas, but it wasn’t a Nas diss record anymore. They needed that record from Jay for the Paid in Full soundtrack and Jay kind of wanted that to be his ‘Who Shot Ya.’ It wasn’t for an album. Remember, originally, ‘Who Shot Ya’ wasn’t on Ready to Die. They put it on there when they remastered it. He just wanted to have a dope record that was just for the streets.
