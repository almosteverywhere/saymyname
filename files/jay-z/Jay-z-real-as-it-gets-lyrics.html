About Real As It Gets

In this song off of Blueprint 3, produced by The Inkredibles, Jay-Z teams up with Young Jeezy to talk about shining and selling drugs

Jay-Z and Jeezy are widely believed to have extensive drug-dealing experience, as opposed to the “resume padder” drug sales of most other rappers

In the Early Days of Rap Genius, Maboo got into hella-trubbs for proclaiming that this is the BEST song off of BP3 (he still thinks it’s the best)
