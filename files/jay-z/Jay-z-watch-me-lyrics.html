About Watch Me

The first collaboration between Jay-Z and Dr. Dre, produced by Irv Gotti and his co-producer Lil' Rob. Later Dr. Dre made a song called “The Watcher” for his 1999 album 2001 and its sequel “The Watcher 2” was found on Jay-Z’s The Blueprint 2 album.
