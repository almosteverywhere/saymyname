About A Star Is Born

This song chronicles everybody who has been a star in the rap game while Jay’s been in it (although not in chronological order). The point is that while everybody has had his time to shine and fade, Jay-Z has been the only constant throughout. He is still on top. So though he’s giving props throughout this song, in doing so he’s underscoring how great he is

Two notable omissions: Game and Cam'ron, both of whom had beef with Jay. Coincidence?

The beat was produced by Kanye West, No I.D. and Kenoe and samples Mother Freedom’s “Touch Me”.
