About Soon You'll Understand

A song rumored to be dedicated to to Trinidadian model Shenelle Scott (with whom tabloids claim Jay has a baby) — written in the final throes of their relationship

2 verses on Jay’s lost love, with a 3rd Freudian verse tying his failed romance to his mom

A rare solo track from 2000’s Dynasty: Roc La Familia album; a time when Jay-Z was becoming increasingly dependent on collaborators like Beanie, Memphis Bleek, etc.

Samples Al Kooper’s “Love Theme” from The Landlord
