About I Just Wanna Love U (Give it 2 Me)

The lead single from Jay Z’s 5th studio album The Dynasty: Roc La Familia and the first Jay-Z single to reach #1 on the Hip-Hop/R&B chart

The chorus samples Rick James' 1981 chart topping dance hit Give It to Me Baby
