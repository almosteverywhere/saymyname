About Can't Get With That

‘Can’t Get With That’ was the B-Side to Jay Z’s first single “In My Lifetime”. Although a video was shot for the song it was not released at the time. The two songs mentioned here were released prior to Jay’s classic debut album “Reasonable Doubt”.
