Hang with me in my MMO
So many places we can go Felicia Day (Ft. The Guild) – Do You Wanna Date My Avatar

MMO stands for massive multiplayer online game; many of these games are also role-playing games or RPGs (combine to get MMORPG). World of Warcraft, Guild Wars and EVE Online are all examples of this genre. The first example is also the most important, as Felicia Day’s experience with WoW are what inspired her to write The Guild in the first place.

MMOs are often expansive places with lots of different locations and content to be played (in order to justify the buying and subscription prices), so one generally has a lot of choices for a first virtual date…

How 'bout, how 'bout a little tank and spank Felicia Day (Ft. The Guild) – Do You Wanna Date My Avatar

“Tank and spank” is a battle strategy common to many MMO and tactics-based RPGs (most notably in boss battles). A “tank” is a character with high HP, aggro (aggravation/enemy attraction) strong defense/healing abilities that can withstand large amounts of damage over a long time period (usually the warrior/soldier class). The tank distracts the enemy while the other party members “spank” it with spells/weapons with a high DPS (damage per second). If needs be, healers may be employed to help keep the tank alive.

Of course, “tank and spank” is a phrase rife with innuendo…
