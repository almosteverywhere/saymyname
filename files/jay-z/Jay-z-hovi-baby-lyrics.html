About Hovi Baby

Just Blaze samples a live version of TLC’s “Digging On You”, and told Complex that he made the beat the day Left Eye died, after hearing the song on the radio being played as part of a tribute.

He also cites it as a precursor to “Show Me What You Got” from a production standpoint.
