About Where I'm From

One of Jay’s most evocative songs, this is a detail-rich — and deeply sad — portrait of the housing projects where he grew up. It has also set off a million arguments over its (unanswered) question: Who’s the best MC — Biggie, Jay-Z, or Nas?
