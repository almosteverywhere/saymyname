About Most Kingz

Jay-Z raps about life at the top of the game. The meme is that “it’s good to be the king” (c.f. Mel Brooks), but it’s also kind of bad to be the king!

The song was inspired by this Basquiat painting:

Which contains the phrase:

Most young kings get their head cut off

The guy from Coldplay brings the hook
