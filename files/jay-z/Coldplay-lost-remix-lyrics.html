About Lost (Remix)

“Lost!” originally appeared on Coldplay’s fourth album Viva la Vida or Death and All His Friends. This new version, “Lost+,” appears on the Prospekt’s March EP and features a verse from Jay-Z (who is good friends with Coldplay’s front man Chris Martin). There was a slight controversy amongst the Coldplay fandom about Jay’s cursing (particularly his use of “niggas”)
