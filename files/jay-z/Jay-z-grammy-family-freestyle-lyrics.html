About "Grammy Family" Freestyle

Hov’s jaw-dropping radio freestyle from around the time of Kingdom Come. He ruminates on success and its costs

Some of these lyrics — far too good to be wasted — would pop up on future songs like “Operation Corporate Takeover”, “Most Kingz”, and even Jay-Z’s collaboration with Coldplay, “Lost+”
