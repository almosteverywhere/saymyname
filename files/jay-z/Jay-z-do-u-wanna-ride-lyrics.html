About Do U Wanna Ride

This song is dedicated to Jay’s friend Emory Jones. Jay wrote the song while Emory was in prison to show him all the good things that were waiting for him outside and make him forget the hard life on jail.

Here’s a clip of Jay speaking on Emory where Jay explains how he feels he lucked out of being caught with Emory.

Jay would eventually help get Emory released from prison
