About Dig A Hole

Dig A Hole is a diss song and largely looked at as a response to Cam'ron’s “You Gotta Love It”.

The original version of this record was produced by Just Blaze over the same instrumental as Fabolous' “Return of the Hustle” and there is no Sterling Simms. There are notable differences between the original’s first and third verses as compared to the standard version. The original leaked some time in 2007.

Beanie Sigel responds to this song in his final line on “Show Must Go On”:

How did we go from crew love to dig a hole?

J.R. Writer also references it on You’re Done (Tru Dyke):

“We put you guy each in ditches yo
Hit with 4, crictical
Jigga know that’s why we did the ho”
