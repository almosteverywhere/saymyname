About Get By (remix)

A Brooklyn’s Finest posse cut remix of Talib Kweli’s “Get By” from the 2002 album “Quality”.

This remix, also produced by Kanye West, samples the soul classic “Sinnerman” by the late musician and activist Nina Simone.
