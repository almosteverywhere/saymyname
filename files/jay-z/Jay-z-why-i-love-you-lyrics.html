About Why I Love You

The closing song of #WTT deals with betrayal. Though names aren’t actually said, you don’t have to BEAN us over the head to get us to make a DASH over to a place where we may be able to figure out the CONSEQUENCE of what they’re talking about

The song is rapped over “I Love You So (I <3 U So)” by Cassius.
