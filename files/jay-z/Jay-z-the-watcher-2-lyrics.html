About The Watcher 2

This is Dre’s sequel to The Watcher from his album 2001 : a sign of Jay’s respect for Dre

Featuring a rare guest verse by Rakim, the song has a decidedly old-head tone. The three are “The Watchers” watching the ghetto, like thug angels
