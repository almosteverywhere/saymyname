About December 4th

This song is Jay-Z’s brief autobiography, with each verse covering a different phase in his life: first, his birth and childhood. Next, his pre-rapping period selling drugs. Finally, he explains his decision to transition from selling drugs to rapping

Jay really gets in touch with his little lost boy, emphasizing his father’s abandonment
