About A Million and One Questions (Remix)

This is a remix to the introduction to Jay-Z’s second album In My Lifetime: Volume 1 and his first album since the deaths of 2Pac and his friend The Notorious B.I.G.

The song gives listeners a glimpse into the pressures of fame that affected the early career of Jay-Z, compared to the more recent Jay-Z who has accepted his role. The song is introspective and lyrical in a way that only Jay-Z can do it
