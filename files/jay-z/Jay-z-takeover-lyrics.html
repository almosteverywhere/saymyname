About Takeover

Jay-Z’s diss song aimed at Mobb Deep and Nas. The song is better remembered for its Nas diss as it catalyzed the long-running and epic Jay-Z/Nas beef

The first 2 verses were debuted live at NYC radio station Hot 97’s Summer Jam concert, complete with childhood picture of Mobb Deep’s Prodigy in 80’s MJ-inspired dance gear

The brilliant beat is sampled from “5 to 1” by the Doors
