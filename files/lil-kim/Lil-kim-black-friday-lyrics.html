About Black Friday

Lil' Kim disses Nicki Minaj (amongst others) over Pharoahe Monch’s “Simon Says” beat

Black Friday premiered on New York’s Hot 97 radio station on Thanksgiving 2010, just a few days after the release of Minaj’s Pink Friday
