About If You Love Me

Lil Kim’s first and heavily anticipated track release of 2012, after she uploaded promotional posters via her Twitter account.

Released on Valentine’s Day, possibly to spite Nicki Minaj in their ongoing beef. Pink Friday: Roman Reloaded was due to be released on the same day until it was pushed back to 3 March.
