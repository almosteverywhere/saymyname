About Triumph

An epic posse cut featuring all nine members of the Wu-Tang Clan (plus Cappadonna), from their second album Forever

“Triumph” was the first single, and it along with it’s classic video, established Wu-Tang as a force in mainstream rap as well as the underground world, while maintaining the dark street sound they introduced on 36 Chambers
