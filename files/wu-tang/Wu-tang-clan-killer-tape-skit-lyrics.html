About Killer Tape Skit

One of the meanings of Wu Tang is Witty Unpredictable Talent And Natural Game, and this is the Wu at their wittiest.

In this skit Raekwon and Method Man are arguing about a lost video tape when Ghostface and U-God show up with news of a drive-by shooting in the neighbourhood.

Despite the dark subject matter, this is a damn funny skit. Listen out for Method Man’s punchline at the end.

Appears before “Wu-Tang: 7th Chamber” on the album.
