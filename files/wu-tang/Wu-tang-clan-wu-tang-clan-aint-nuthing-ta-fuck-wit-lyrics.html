About Wu-Tang Clan Ain't Nuthing ta Fuck Wit

The song came out in 1993, on the Wu Tang Clan’s debut album Enter the Wu-Tang (36 Chambers).

Produced by RZA
Featuring RZA, Inspectah Deck and Method Man

Interestingly the famous sample (the du du du du du)is built around a old cartoon about a super powered dog
