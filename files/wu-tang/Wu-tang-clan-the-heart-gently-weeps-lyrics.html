About The Heart Gently Weeps

Rza, always been a fan of the beatles wanted to incorporate their music into a wu project. While on the set of American Gangster, Russell Crowe gave Rza a Gretsch guitar from 1961, the kind of guitar the late George Harrison from the beatles played.

The Guitar and Erykah Badu’s parts are based on The Beatles' 1968 song “While My Guitar Gently Weeps”. John Frusciante from Red Hot Chili Peppers performed lead guitar and Dhani Harrison, George Harrison’s son performed Rhythm Guitar with his late father’s guitar that Rza was given.
