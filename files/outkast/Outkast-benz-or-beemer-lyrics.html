About Benz or Beemer

Benz or Beamer

Released in 1995…Under the New Jersey Drive Soundtrack. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Jersey_Drive,_Vol._1

Typical Outkast classic, trading verses back and forth over a dope Organized Noize beat.

‘Kast raps about jackin’ (Robbery), mary jane, the struggle, car jackings…and whips, of course.
