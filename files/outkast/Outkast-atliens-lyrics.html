About ATLiens

Fuck. Where to begin. Well, this song has an absolutely bangin beat. Second, the verses provided by Big Boi and Andre are, as usual, outstanding. And then…

THE CHORUS! A stunningly perfect chorus summarizing the ethos of the Southern rap lifestyle. And it’s structure — the last line (the O-YAY-ER) emerges perfectly, surprisingly rhyming with the first and second line — we would have expected the last line to rhyme with the third, but it rhymes with the other two. But, of course, to recognize exactly why this is so amazing you have to listen to it. So stop reading my shit and start bangin!
