About Hate Me Now

Ever wonder what it would sound like if Nas rapped about his wealth over a classical piece while Diddy shouted obscenities at you? Well, wonder no longer!

“Hate Me Now”, from Nas' 1999 album I Am…, peaked at #2 on the Billboard charts, and the video for the song became a point of controversy

In the original cut of the video, both Nas and Diddy were shown crucified. Diddy wanted his scene taken out, but when MTV first aired the video, his scene was still included. Diddy then went Juvenile on Nas' manager
