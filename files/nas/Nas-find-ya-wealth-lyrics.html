About Find Ya Wealth

This is song is a perfect example that notes the difference between Nas and his counterpart Jay-z. While Jay-z boasts on his wealth and riches, Nas aims to promote self-awareness and self-improvement by telling us to dig deep and find the wealth within ourselves.

Nas has always been on a higher level than Jay-Z. Even though Jay-Z might be a savvy investor, he is not in the same poetic and moral level as Nas. Esco has always remained true to his roots and true to himself, never letting go of his street poet essence.

Nas > Jay-Z
