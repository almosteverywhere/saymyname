About Come Get Me

DJ Premier samples “We’re Just Trying to Make It” by The Persuaders, as well as Nas’s own voice from the Mobb Deep collab “It’s Mine”

This is the 6th and longest song from Nas' “Nastradamus”, in which Nas addresses his haters as well as rival rappers, announcing for them to “Come Get Me” (Cue “Takeover”)
