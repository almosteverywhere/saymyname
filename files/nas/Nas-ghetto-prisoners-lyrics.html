About Ghetto Prisoners

Track 7 on Nas’s ‘I Am…’ album, “Ghetto Prisoners” focuses on overcoming oppression and adversity and remaining optimistic even though the future looks bleak. This song contains some of Nas’s best verses on the whole album, using metaphysical, spiritual, 5%er, and even historical lines and references to convey his ideas.
