About Accident Murderers

A track about felony murder — a (some would say, racist) rule in the majority of states saying that if anyone dies during a felony (like, the dude has a heart attack while you are robbing him) you get charged with murder…

Q: What if it was an accident?!

This track was produced by No I.D., samples “They Said It Couldn’t Be Done” by Norman Feels and “The Bridge” by MC Shan, and is notable for featuring the only other rapper on the album besides Nasir. As with all the art for the LP, the photo was taken by Matthew Salacuse and treated by Alex Haldi.
