About Dispear

The title of the song is a double entendre:

1) The title refers to dis-spear, as in this spear, which is a reference to the recurring metaphor used in the song.

2) The title also refers to despair, which phonetically sounds similar to dispear. Additionally, the ideas in the track are about the despair of “the masses,” or the disadvantaged people.
