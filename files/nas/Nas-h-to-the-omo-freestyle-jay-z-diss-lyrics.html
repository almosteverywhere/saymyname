About H to the OMO (Freestyle Jay-Z Diss)

This Freestyle is directed toward Jay-Z and his Roc-a-fella crew right before the first round of diss tracks released.

It was untitled at first because it was a radio freestyle but was then called ‘'H to the OMO’‘ interpreting Jay-Z’s song ’‘Izzo(HOVA)’‘, which makes out ’‘HOMO’‘ instead of ’‘HOVA’‘.
