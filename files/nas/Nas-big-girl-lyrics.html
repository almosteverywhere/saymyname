About Big Girl

Nastradamus is the fourth studio album by American rapper Nas, released November 23, 1999 on Columbia Records in the United States.

The song describes a relationship with a girl who already has a partner, who isn’t very interested in becoming serious with her.
Nas then describes how he can treat her better, and all the things he can do that nobody else can to make her feel loved.
He then questions their relationship at the end of the song, and asks her what she wants from him, citing its all up to her because she is a big girl now.
