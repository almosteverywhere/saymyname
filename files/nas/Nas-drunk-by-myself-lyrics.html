About Drunk By Myself

Nas gets up close and personal with us and enlightens us about his depression episode, confessing his inner grim feelings.

He reports his life is derailing in a big way as everything goes wrong simultaneously from difficulties holding up relationship with years-long fiancee Carmen to his so called friends up in his entourage not to mention the haters.

Although he could fight it off, applying patch after patch on his failing life he rather not doing anything at all.

One a side note, Nas draws a parallel between himself and a drunk driver throughout the song from the beginning when he decides to go for a drive to the end when he hardly makes his way home.

Far-fetched? Not exact one bit, we’re all drivers of our own lives, aren’t we?.

The keyword is alcohol which both Nas and the drunk driver share in common and makes them a potential threats for other peoples…….
