About Biterphobia

“Soul Intent” was an Underground Detroit rap group in the early to mid 1990s who were very important to the development of MC Eminem’s Career. Previously called Bassmint Productions The group consisted of rapper M&M (now Eminem), DJ Butterfingers, Chaos Kid (now Snafu), Manix (Butterfingers' brother), And Vitamin C (MC Big Proof).“ "Bitterphobia” was the B-side off their first single “Fuckin Backstabber” Displays the early development of one of the most ill-est lyricists of our time, while showing off his phenomenal Flowing skills
