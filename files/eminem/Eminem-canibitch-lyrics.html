About Canibitch

This song is a part of the beef between Eminem and Canibus. Both are great lyricists, and is the most evenly matched of Eminem’s many beefs. While Em name drops Canibus with subtle disses in “Square Dance”, “When the Music Stops”, and “Say What You Say”, this is his first full song dedicated to dissing Canibus, where he tells the fictional account of how the beef was started (and finished).
