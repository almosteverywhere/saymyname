About 3 a.m.

3 A.M. is the second official single on Eminem’s album: Relapse.
In this song Eminem raps out being a serial killer, rapping from the perspective of his alter ego Slim Shady. The song was released on the iTunes Store on April 28, 2009. Say what you want about the Relapse album but he killed this shit.
