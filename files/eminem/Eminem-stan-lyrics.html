About Stan

“Stan,” a series of letters from an increasingly unhinged Eminem fan, is one of Em’s best received songs of all time. It placed #15 on VH1’s top 100 hip-hop songs of all time.

This track disproved the critics who claimed Em lacked talent and relied on shock tactics. Eminem was able to display maturity throughout the whole song and proved to a lot of doubters that he could be successful without attempting to be controversial.

The track (produced by DJ Mark the 45 King, of “Hard Knock Life” fame) samples British singer Dido’s record “Thank You”
