About Crack a Bottle

From Eminem’s album sixth studio album Relapse, this song went on to win the Grammy Award for Best Performance by a Duo or Group in 2010

Before it’s official release on iTunes (used to drum up hype for Relapse), a reference track leaked. It featured Eminem performing Dre’s verse first, then his, both of them being very similar to the final cut. He then performs a 3rd verse, rumoured to be ghostwritten for 50 Cent, who appears on the final track albeit with a completely different verse.
