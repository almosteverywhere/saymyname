About Control (Kendrick Lamar Control Response)

Eminem made a response to Kendrick Lamar’s game-disrupting verse on Big Sean’s song “Control.” The ridiculousness of this verse shows that this is just for fun, and Eminem is playfully jabbing at the popularity / infamy of Kendrick’s verse.
