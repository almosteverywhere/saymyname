About 2.0 Boys

The talented Slaughterhouse quadruplet of Royce Da 5'9", Joell Ortiz, Joe Budden, and Crooked I come together with Yelawolf and Slim Shady himself for a posse cut built for Rap Genius to reverse-engineer

The title is a reference to the fact that Em’s label Shady Records recently revamped their roster. They dumped unsuccessful acts like Stat Quo and Bobby Creekwater, and are rebooting with Slaughterhouse and Yelawolf

Produced by Yelawolf’s in-house producer, Will Power.
