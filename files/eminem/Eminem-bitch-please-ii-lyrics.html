About Bitch Please II

“Bitch Please II” is a track from Eminem’s sophomore LP The Marshall Mathers LP. It’s a sequel to a Snoop Dogg track (featuring Xzibit and Nate Dogg) that appeared on his 1999 album No Limit Top Dogg, and features the aforementioned artists, plus Dr. Dre and Shady himself.
