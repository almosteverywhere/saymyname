About Bad Influence

‘Bad Influence’ was on the ‘End Of Days’ soundtrack.

In this song, Eminem states that he realises he is a role model to kids but, to paraphrase what he says in interviews, ‘it’s up to the parents to check what their kids are listening to’.
He tells kids to jump off a bridge and shoot themselves just because he says so, mockingly acting as the ‘bad role model’ people categorize him as.
