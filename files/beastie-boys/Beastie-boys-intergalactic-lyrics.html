About Intergalactic

“Intergalactic" is track #7 on Beastie Boys 5th (full-length) studio album, Hello Nasty, released 7.14.1998. In addition to producing, Mario Caldato, Jr. also shares a writing credit with the Boys. It was dropped as a single on 5.12.1998, the group’s 3rd Top 40 single, hitting #28 on the Billboard Hot 100 charts and #5 on the UK Singles Chart — the Beasties' biggest hit in that country. “Intergalactic" received the Grammy Award for Best Rap Performance by a Duo or Group in 1999.

Source: typophile

  • “Prelude C# Minor“ composed by Rachmaninoff and performed by Lex Baxter
