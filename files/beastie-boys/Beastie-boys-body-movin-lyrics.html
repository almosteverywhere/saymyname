About Body Movin'

“Body Movin'” is Track #6 on Beastie Boys' fifth (full-length) studio album, Hello Nasty. It was the record’s second single, released on 11.5.1998. Length is 3:09.

Samples include:

  • “Oye Como Va“ by Tito Puente from the album, Oye Como Va: the Dance Collection (1997)

  • “Modern Dynamic Physical Fitness Activities“ by Ed Durlacher

Beastiemania reports that “Body Movin’” has been performed in 178 known concerts.

  • First Known Performance: 6.20.1998 — Lorely, Germany
    https://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_detailpage&v=Fs_rFPHEoIQ
  • Last Known Performance: 6.12.2009 — Great Stage Park, Manchester, TN, US

Above, “Body Movin’ Poster” — released in 1998 to coincide with the "Body Movin” single.
Dimensions: 46cm x 61cm (18” x 24”)
