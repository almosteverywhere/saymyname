About Don't Play No Game That I Can't Win

“Don’t Play No Game That I Can’t Win” is Track 7 on Beastie Boys' 8th (full-length) studio album, Hot Sauce Committee Part Two, which was…

  • Written by: Beastie Boys and Santi White, aka Santigold
  • Additional Mixing by: Switch
  • Published by: Brooklyn Dust Music/Universal-Polygram International Publishing, Inc. (ASCAP)/Little Jerk (ASCAP)
  • Saxophone by: Neal Usatin

The album’s release was delayed until 4.27.2011 due to MCA’s diagnosis of salivary cancer. The song was HSC2’s 4th single, issued on 7.26.2011. It was the last Beastie Boys single before the death of Adam Yauch. “Don’t Play No Game That I Can’t Win“ was never performed live in Concert.

source: llayne777
