About Long Burn the Fire

Eighth track from the Beastie Boy’s 2011 album Hot sauce Committe Part Two. Part 1 was delayed indefinitely after group memeber Adam “MCA” Yauch was diagnosed with cancer in 2009. His health condition was treated and in 2011 Hot Sauce…Part 2 was released with virtually the same tracklist as Part 1.
