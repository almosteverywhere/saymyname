About Honky Rink

This is a B-Side for the “Gratitude” 12”. Recorded and mixed at G-Son Studios, Atwater Village, CA “Honky Rink” was mastered by Tom Baker at Future Disc. As the fourth single for CYH, it dropped on 10.4.1992.

Honky Rink, was performed in 4 known concerts.

  • 1st Known Performance: 5.25.2007 — The Crocodile Cafe, Seattle, WA, US
    http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FpHM958zYGo
  • Last known Performance: 7.1.2007 — Babie Doly, Gdynia, Poland
