About Ch-Check It Out

No, for real, check this out.
This is track #1 on the To the 5 Boroughs LP, released 6.4.2004. Recorded & mixed at Oscilloscope Laboratories, Beastie Boys get writing and production credit, with Mix Master Mike on turntables. Recording engineered by Beastie Boys and Jon Weiner. Mixed by Supa Engineer “DURO” for No Question Ent./Loreal Inc. Mastered by Chris Athens at Sterling Sound.

source: musicstack

Samples include:

  • “The O.J. Chronicles” — an episode of "The Joe Frank Show” by Joe Frank & David Cross

Source: sonsofstevegarvey

Beastiemania reports that “Ch-Check It Out” has been performed in 117 known concerts.

  • 1st Known Performance: 5.10.2004 — Club Maria, Berlin, Germany
