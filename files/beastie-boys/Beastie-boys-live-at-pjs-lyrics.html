About Live at P.J.'s

“Live at P.J.’s" is Track 16 on Beastie Boys' third (full-length) LP Check Your Head. Writing credit is shared by Beastie Boys & Money Mark Nishita. It was performed by:

  • Adam Yauch (bass)
  • Michael Diamond (drums)
  • Adam Horovitz (guitar)
  • Mark Nishita (D6, Clav, Organ, Wurlitzer)
  • James Bradley, Jr. (percussion)

“Live at P.J.’s" was recorded and mixed at G-Son Studios, Atwater Village, CA, with Mario Caldato, Jr. producing and engineering. After sequencing at PCP Labs, the album was released on 4.21.1992.

The song was performed in 52 known concerts.

  • 1st Known Performance: 5.17.1992 — Michigan State University, East Lansing, MI, US
