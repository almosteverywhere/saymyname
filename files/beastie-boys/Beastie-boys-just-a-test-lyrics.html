About Just a Test

Track 5 on Beastie Boys’ 5th (full-length) studio album, Hello Nasty. The album was produced by Beastie Boys and Mario Caldato, Jr., who also served as engineer. It was recorded at:

  • G-Son Studios (LA)
  • The Dungeon (NYC)
  • The Tree House (NYC)
  • Ted Diamond’s House of Hits (NYC)
  • Soho Music Studios (NYC)
  • Dessau Studios (NYC)
  • RPM Studios (NYC).

Also engineered and mixed by Steve Revitte and Sue Dyer at RPM Studios (NYC), the album was mastered by Howie Weinberg and Andy van Dette at Masterdisk (NYC). Hello Nasty was released 7.14.1998.

Samples include:

  • Excerpts from “The Pair Extraordinare” by The Pair Extraordinare

“Just a Test” was never performed live in concert.
