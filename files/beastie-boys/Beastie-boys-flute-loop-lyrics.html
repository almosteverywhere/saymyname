About Flute Loop

This is the 13th song on Beastie Boys’ fourth (full-length) studio album, Ill Communication, released 5.31.1994. Writing credit is shared by Beastie Boys, Mario Caldato, Jr., & Al Kooper. The track was recorded and mixed at G-Son Studios, Atwater Village, CA & Tin Pan Alley, New York, NY. It was produced and engineered by Mario C and assistant engineer, Settly. “Flute Loop” was sequenced at Bundy’s Playhouse and mastered by Tom Baker at Futuredisc.

Samples include:

  • “Flute Thing“ by The Blues Project (Al Kooper) from the album, Projections (1966)
  • “I Spy Cops” by Richard Pryor, from the album, Craps (After Hours) (1971)

“Flute Loop” was performed in 146 known concerts.

  • 1st Known Performance: 6.22.1994 — Astoria Theatre, London, England
