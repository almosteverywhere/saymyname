About Egg Man

This is Track 4 on Paul’s Boutique, released 7.25.1989 on Capitol Records. Beastie Boys & Dust Brothers share writing and production credits. Engineered by Mario Caldato, Jr. and assisted by Allen Abrahamson, “Egg Man" was recorded at Mario G’s and mixed at Record Plant.

The song is inspired by the Beasties' love for throwing eggs at people, whether unsuspecting passers-by, or people who deserved it. It’s a practice they indulged in from their days as a NY Hardcore band right up until the making of Paul’s Boutique, and possibly afterward…
