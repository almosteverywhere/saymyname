About Netty's Girl

“Netty’s Girl” first appeared on the “Pass the Mic” 12' single, released 4.7.1992. It was recorded and mixed at G-Son Studios, Atwater Village, CA, and mastered by Tom Baker at Future Disc. The video was directed by Mike’s very tolerant wife, Tammy D. Beastiemania reports that it was performed in 1 known concert:

  • 2.2.2005 — Festival Hall, Melbourne, Australia
