About Gratitude

“Gratitude” is the 4th track on Beastie Boys' third (full-length) LP, Check Your Head. It was recorded and mixed at G-Son Studios, Atwater Village, CA, with Mario Caldato, Jr. producing and engineering. After sequencing at PCP Labs, the album was released on 4.21.1992. As the fourth single for CYH, “Gratitude” dropped on 10.4.1992, appealing primarily to modern rock radio. MCA uses a distortion pedal on his bass guitar to create the track’s signature sound.

source: eil.com

The music video to this song is an homage to the 1972 concert film, Pink Floyd: Live at Pompeii and was recorded in Rotorua, New Zealand. The video was featured on MTV’s “Beavis and Butthead,” who gave the song a positive review. This song was also included in Guitar Hero 5.

source: musicstack
