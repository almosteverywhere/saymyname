About Best Friend

This song actually has a decent story — its not just about going out, spending money, getting girls, etc… It’s about a guy who has a close girl friend who he wishes was his girlfriend. He talks about all the different things he’d like to do for her and it’s not all sexual stuff. But she’s always with some guy and he is wondering if they are just friends or something more
