About Candy Shop

Candy Shop by 50 Cent ft. Olivia is easily one of his best known hits. Hitting number 1 on the Billboard’s Hot 100 back in 2005 when it came out, and nominated for a Grammy in 2006.

It mainly about getting a girl, or boy, back to the crib and having all types of hot, kinky sex, and the lyrics contain all sorts of sexual innuendos. Some of the innuendos include but are not limited to: “candy shop”, “lollipop”, “hit the spot”, “wanna taste”, “magic stick”, and the most popular “I melt in your mouth girl, not in your hand”

All in all the song is a classic club or love making song.
