About Be a Gentleman

A 2000 dis by 50 of Jay-Z. 50 had first dissed Jay (along with pretty much every other rapper alive) in his song “How To Rob”, and Jay had responded with the immortal line, “I’m about a dollar, what the fuck is 50 cents?” on his song “It’s Hot (Some Like It Hot)”. This song is 50’s shot back. The two would continue to have a rocky relationship for years, even after 50’s mega-fame
