About 187 Yayo

187 Yayo is a song performed by “50 Cent,” featuring “Tony Yayo” on the hip hop compilation album, Eminem Presents: The Re-Up. It was produced by DJ Whoo Kid and
Sha Money XL, appearing on the album as Track 8.
