About Fuck The Police

“Fuck tha Police” takes the form of a trial against the LAPD, with Dr. Dre, MC Ren, and Ice Cube as Judge, Court Officer, and Witness

The song pissed off the feds: N.W.A. is reported to have been monitored by the FBI — gangstaaa

But N.W.A. was responding to LAPD brutality that culminated in the Rodney King beating in 1991 and the South Central riots in 1992

ps Spiderman sides with NWA on this one..
