About Niggaz 4 Life

This song is addressed to the critics who oppose NWA’s frequent use of the word “nigga.” They give six reasons for its continued use (assuming each individual member speaks for the group):

1) because of how often the police harass them;

2) because it fits in the with rest of their dirty language;

3) because of the size of their dicks;

4) because they will always be black;

5) because their lyrics are so excellent; and

6) because “it’s just the way shit has to be.”

Later on the album, in “The Dayz of Wayback,” a seventh reason is given:

7) because of their pasts.
