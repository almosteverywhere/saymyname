About 100 Miles and Runnin' (Clean Version)

This is the clean version of “100 Miles and Runnin'”. Although they say “hell” and “damn,” they don’t say “nigga” or “motherfucker,” which are typically replaced by “brother” and “young brother” in order to keep the number of syllables the same.

Looks like Dre does give a fuck about radio play.
