About One Less Bitch

Track 11 on N.W.A’s final 1991 album Niggaz4Life (also known as Efil4zaggin) — “One Less Bitch” — puts members MC Ren and Dr. Dre in the gritty streets of Compton, and both have serious dilemmas with women. In this song specifically, Dre and Ren both detail their experiences with the women they see; some are prostitutes, and others married to law enforcement — regardless, at the end of each verse, Ren and Dr.Dre both get rid of of their issues with women… By exploiting and killing them. This builds on the continuing theme the album has on being a “nigga”, doing stereotypical “nigga” things, and what such a thing means in context.

“One Less Bitch” samples both Tom Browne — Funkin' for Jamaica (N.Y.) and Barry White — I’m Gonna Love You Just a Little More Baby.
