About I'm Different

This song is from his highly anticipated latest mixtape “187”, a pre-album mixtape to promote his upcoming album “Hotel California” on April 9th.

This song originally was a freestyle but he recorded it as a real song.

The beat is sampled from 2 Chainz “I’m Different” song.
