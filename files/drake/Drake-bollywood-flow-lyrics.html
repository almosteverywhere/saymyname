About Bollywood Flow

On June 10, 2010, Drake appeared on the Tim Westwood Radio Show to spit an exclusive freestyle. About a third of the song was used on Lil' Wayne’s “Right Above It” after some changes. So this song is actually called Drake- “Tim Westwood Freestyle”.

But in Overall, I just gotta give it up to Drake, man. He kills this. Well it’s original version doesn’t have a beat. So thats mostly the reason this version, “Bollywood Flow”, is waayyy better.
