About Come Winter

From his debut mixtape “Room for Improvement” (2006)

Drake speaks on the time of the year most affectionately termed “cuffing season” usually encompassing the colder months in Autumn and Winter when people rush to couple up and stay warm. This usually fades out as soon as the weather starts getting warmer.
