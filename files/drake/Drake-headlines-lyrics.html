About Headlines

Features production from Boi-1da and Noah “40″ Shebib — brilliant song, subtle lyrics..

Interestingly, throughout the lyrics, headlines are never mentioned..

https://twitter.com/#!/drakkardnoir/status/97544387017441280

Drake says the song was inspired by the blog Mostly Junk Food — rap analysis blog Keep It Fr3sh had a hand in it as well..
