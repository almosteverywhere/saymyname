About Club Paradise

This song shows Drizzy feeling (surprise!) melancholy about his success and the toll it’s taken on those he left behind. Hmm, that sounds somehow familiar..

“Club Paradise” is Drake’s favorite strip club in Toronto (he’s also a fan of “For Your Eyes Only” and “Brass Rail”) — it’s also the most shady….

The chorus features backup vocals by The Weeknd.

If you think this song is too soft, you ought to consult the Big Ghost Review, nahmean?

Club Paradise, Drake’s tour with A$AP Rocky and Kendrick Lamar, is strictly an exhibition of the music Drake likes. No big names or Young Money affiliates, just Take Care, section.80, and LiveLoveA$AP.
