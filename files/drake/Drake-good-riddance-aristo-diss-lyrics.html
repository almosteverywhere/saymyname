About Good Riddance (Aristo Diss)

This was probaly one of Drake’s last diss tracks after he stated he didn’t have time to respond to anymore beef. Everyone featured on this diss track has been affected by Aristo in some way. Aristo was really never famous. Neither was Drake (at the time this track came out). Drake single handedly retired Aristo no doubt. The last line Drake raps pretty much sums up the whole reason for this track “Good Riddance Aristo I Just Retired You!”
