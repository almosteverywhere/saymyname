About Forever

“Forever” paints a 4-part chronological portrait of the career of a rapper:

Drake is young and hungry, eager to be successful and famous for a long time, and bragging about his success so far in spite of haters and record labels who wouldn’t sign him

Kanye is at the top of the game, but sick of the drawbacks of fame — the paparazzi, the hangers-on, etc.

Lil Wayne is also at the top of the game and wants to stay there, which he figures he can do pretty easily if he keeps doing what he’s doing

Eminem’s recent album Relapse came out almost 5 years after the release of his previous album. During that time he suffered from chronic depression, succumbed to numerous drug problems, and fell off the rap map. Eminem is back and hungry to prove himself again. He does so by killing 3 of the hottest rappers in the game on this song with a masterful verse
