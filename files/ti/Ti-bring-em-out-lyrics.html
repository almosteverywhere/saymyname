About Bring Em Out

Bring ‘Em Out is from T.I.’s 2004 album “Urban Legend”. It is a diss song, directed to his haters in general, and references several times his recent jail santence, the first of 3. This track samples Jay-Z’s “What More Can I Say” (2003).
