About Act II: T.I.

This is a track from T.I.’s album that went platinum T.I. vs T.I.P. The whole point of the album is to express both sides of Clifford Joseph Harris Jr..

This track specifies his suave, level-minded CEO personality named T.I.. On the song, T.I. faces an confusing scenario where his producers and partners are allegating he did something he doesn’t remember.
