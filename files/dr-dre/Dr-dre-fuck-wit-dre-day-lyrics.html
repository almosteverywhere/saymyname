About Fuck Wit Dre Day

A tripartite diss directed at Dre’s ex-NWA mate Eazy-E, East Coast rapper Tim Dog (of Fuck Compton fame) and 2 Live Crew leader Luke (who was known as Luke Skyywalker before Lucas sued)
