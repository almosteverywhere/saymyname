About Bitches Ain't Shit

Just where you think you’d find some meme-rap misogyny, you find Dre complaining about being left for a white man (Jerry Heller), Snoop narrating a forlorn tale of betrayal, and then (female rapper) Jewell deconstructing the phrase “on my nuts”! One of the greatest Gangsta Rap songs of all time.
