About Eyez Closed

Song from Snoop Dogg’s 11th studio album, Doggumentary. A meditative track that addresses hardships in life and the pressure one faces on a daily basis.

The song was produced by Kanye West, Jeff Bhasker and Mike Dean. It features a sample from Jimmy Spicer’s Money (Dollar Bill Y'all).
