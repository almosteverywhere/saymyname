About Broken Glass

Broken Glass is on the “Beautiful Struggle” Album, released in 2004.

Samples: The Message by Grandmaster Flash and The Furious Five feat. Grandmaster Melle Mel and Duke Bootee (1982)

It tells the story of a small-town girl who moves to New York only to watch her life fall apart. Talib really flexes his story-telling muscle on this one.
