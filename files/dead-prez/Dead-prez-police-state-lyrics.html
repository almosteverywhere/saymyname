About Police State

A police state is one in which the government exercises rigid and repressive controls over the social, economic, and political life of the population. A police state typically exhibits elements of totalitarianism and social control, and there is usually little or no distinction between the law and the exercise of political power by the executive. (Wikipedia)

In their politically-conscious style of hip-hop, Dead Prez repeatedly call attention to the way in which Black America exists as a police state within mainstream US society. From the prison system to the school system, the lives of African Americans are restricted and exploited through US government policies. In this song, they call for a violent revolution against this systematic oppression.
