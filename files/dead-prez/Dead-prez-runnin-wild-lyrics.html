About Runnin' Wild

The first track off the DJ Green Lantern-hosted, 2009 mixtape Pulse Of The People — Turn Off The Radio Vol. 3. Between quotes from Mumia Abu-Jamal, stic.man and M1 describe the life of a Young Black Male growing up in ‘09. A glimpse into the highschool personas of the lyricists, the youth they describe fits the Revolutionary But Gangsta title perfectly: he packs a gun incase he has to defend himself from a cop, and flips off his teachers when They Schools instruct him to follow in Obama’s footsteps. Through this character, Dead Prez demonstrates the development of the anti-establishment left-politics that permeates the rest of the tape.
