About Don't Hate My Grind

Released during The Great Recession of 2009, this Bub B-assisted track meditates on the problems average people struggle with in a society that favors the rich, and the strategies they develop for getting over. Borrowing Melle Mel’s oft-quoted phrase from The Message, stic.man and M1 emphasize the tension that builds in desperate circumstances, and defend the illegal (and potentially militant) behavior that results.
