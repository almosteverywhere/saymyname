About Poetic Justice

K.Dot has been dropped off back at home by his homies and is about to go see Sherane. He’s probably driving on the way there in his Mother’s van. He talks about her and their relationship so far — it appears they may have had some arguments, he talks about her meeting up with her girlfriends to curse him, and going out partying rather than talking with him. Watch the music video here.

Also samples Janet Jackson’s “Any Time, Any Place” http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jnWPmk76PcU

Janet, of course, starred in the movie Poetic Justice

http://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_detailpage&v=kdZJhiuXZvs#t=207s Producer Scoop Deville on Dj City’s Behind the Beat, talking about the production behind this song.
