About HiiiPower

The first song released off Kendrick Lamar’s Section.80, HiiiPoWeR talks of self enlightenment through reflection and active work. It constantly touches on racial subjects, figures and conspiracy theories of today and the past.

As described at the end of “Cut You Off (To Grow Closer)” (From his mixtape, O.D.), Hiii Power is a way of thinking with higher expectations, and achieving richness in body and mind. Essentially a quest for knowledge and wisdom of the body, mind and spirit.

The song was produced by J. Cole, samples “Duration Pt. 9” by Samtoo, and was mixed by Top Dawg Entertainment’s engineer, Ali. It also features background vocals by Alori Joh
