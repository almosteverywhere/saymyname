About County Building Blues

Bonus track off Kendrick Lamar’s excellent major label debut LP good kid, mA.A.d city

As the introduction to this track reveals, this song, along with the leaked “Cartoon & Cereal” was most likely going to be the opener to the album itself, before being cut from the album’s main narrative
