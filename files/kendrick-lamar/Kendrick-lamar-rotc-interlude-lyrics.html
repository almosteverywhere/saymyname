About R.O.T.C (Interlude)

Here lies the thoughts of Kendrick Lamar on your typical early morning in June.

Since the money is not coming as he had wished, K-Dot ponders whether or not he should discontinue his rapping career and restart his life as a drug dealer, some deep thoughts here by the Compton-born wordsmith. The title plays a clever significance as well.

The outro of the song is sung by B.J The Chicago Kid, and it covers the vocals of Billy Caldwell on “Open Your Eyes.” This song was famously known in the hip-hop world as the sample that Common used in The Light
