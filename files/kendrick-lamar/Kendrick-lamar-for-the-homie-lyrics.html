About For The Homie

For the HOMIES

Kendrick released this song online on July 30, 2010. It served as a warm-up for his Overly Dedicated mixtape released later in the year, but it was not featured on the final release.

The content of the song describes the day-to-day lifestyle and activities of his friends.

The song is a sequel to the track “For the Girlfriends”, which was released on December 22, 2009 as a warm-up for the Kendrick Lamar EP.

Produced by Sounwave
