About G Code

2009 mixtape C4 which is inspired by Lil’ Wayne’s 2008 album The Carter 3 comprising mostly of freestyles over instrumentals from Wayne’s album in addition to a number of original tracks. Kendrick’s re imagination of Wayne’s album is a very insightful listen into the evolution of his style, as well as drawing up an interesting comparison between the two artists.
This particular beat was the beat for Lil Wayne song Let the beat build.
