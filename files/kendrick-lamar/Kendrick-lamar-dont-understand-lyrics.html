About Don't Understand

In the beginning of the song, Kendrick’s boy who we’ll call Jimmy, has a brother who was murdered. Jimmy’s emotionally distraught, so he decides he’s gonna go find his brother’s killer. Kendrick tries to calm him down, but Jimmy tells him that he doesn’t understand how he’s feeling.

In the second verse Kendrick is describing the cycle of violence. Kids that kill are kids that are corrupted. Kendrick uses Jimmy to make this verse more applicable an understandable to us, the listeners.

In the third verse Jimmy comes back fifteen minutes after leaving to go kill his brother’s murderers. He doesn’t directly say that he killed his brother’s murderers, but its obvious because he tells Kendrick to hide his guns. Kendrick tells Jimmy he’s there for him and then he goes to sleep.

The next day Kendrick wakes up with a cold. He goes to rite aid and when he goes to the parking lot the crew of the boy Jimmy killed is there.

This song samples Grant Green’s “Maybe Tomorrow”.
