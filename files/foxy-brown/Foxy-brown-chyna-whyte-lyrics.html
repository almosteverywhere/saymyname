About Chyna Whyte

“Chyna White” is the first song from Foxy Brown’s sophomore album Chyna Doll which was released in 1999 on Def Jam. This album went platinum.
“Chyna White” is a song where Foxy is saying how she great she is lyrically. In the song, Foxy uses allegory; she plays the character of Chyna White, a woman who is symbolic of the nature of heroin dealing in the hood, a drug that was really popular in the ‘60’s and '70’s, and was also a precursor to the crack epidemic of the '80’s. In this song, she also alludes to the time period of the heroin phenomenon, which was also the same period when the original Foxy Brown movie was released, starring Pam Grier. The original Foxy Brown obviously inspired the rapper; she only took her performance name from the movie.
