About Slangin Birds

This song came off of 2 Chainz’s mixtape “Tru Realigion”, which was released in November of 2011. It is track 11 on the mixtape.

The song features Young Jeezy and Yo Gotti. Birdman is on the 4th verse.

The song was produced by Drumma Boy
