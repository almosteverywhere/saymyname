About Villuminati

Track #1 on Born Sinner

This song samples the intro from R. Kelly’s I Wish remix along with The Notorious B.I.G.’s Juicy

The song sets the mood and theme for most of the album; As he states, “it’s way darker this time.” It addresses his different themes of good and evil (as the picture of both devil horns and a halo symbolizes), and his styles of rapping; bragging, being real, or using crazy flow… or a little bit of each.
