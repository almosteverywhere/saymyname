About Dreams

Here J. Cole is talks about one girl he really liked but he didn’t even know her that well, which is why he questions himself throughout the song as to weather he’s crazy or not.

Probably one of the most lyrical/stalker-ish songs ever.

This song also samples Hank Crawford’s “Wildflower.”
