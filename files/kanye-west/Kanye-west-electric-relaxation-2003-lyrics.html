About Electric Relaxation 2003

This track is a remix of Electric Relaxation on Consequence’s 2005 mixtape, “A Tribe Called Quence”

Consequence (born Dexter Mills) is the cousin of ATCQ’s Q-Tip and former member of Kanye West’s G.O.O.D. Music affiliation.
