About Crack Music

  • From Kanye West’s second album release Late Registration.

  • Features Game, but only on the hook.

  • Theme of the song is comparing the crack cocaine epidemic in America to the addiction of hip-hop in America. Also talks about conspiracy theories that have to do with politics.

  • Produced by Kanye West, with The Game, Keyshia Cole, Charlie Wilson, and Tony Williams providing backing vocals.

  • Samples “Since You Came in My Life” as performed by New York Community Choir
