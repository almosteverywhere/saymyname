About Chain Heavy

The relative weight of chains, both figurative and literal, is explored in great depth on this G.O.O.D. Friday entry

For the first time, Kanye’s verses, albeit fiery, can’t quite compare to those around them. Lyrical master Talib Kweli demonstrates the art of a guest appearance, while G.O.O.D. Music signee Consequence delivers arguably the best bars of the song
