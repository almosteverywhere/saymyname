About Devil in a New Dress

Produced by Bink, Devil In a New Dress finds Kanye using familiar religious imagery in contemplating all kinds of nasty thoughts about his girl

It’s perhaps an ode to Amber Rose, Kanye’s (now ex-) girlfriend and recent muse, whose figure could have only been forged in the Depths of Hell by The Evil One (in a good way..)

The title, of course, is a pun on the famous Mitch Ryder and the Detroit Wheels song “Devil with a Blue Dress On”

Samples vocals and melody from the classic Carole Kings’s love song “Will You Still Love Me Tomorrow” as performed by Smokey Robinson.

This song is also known for Ross' outstanding verse, outshining Kanye on his own track.
