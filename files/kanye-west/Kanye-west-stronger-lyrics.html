About Stronger

The biggest hit from Kanye’s Graduation, “Stronger” samples French techno-pop group Daft Punk’s “Harder, Better, Faster, Stronger” (which in turn samples “Cola Bottle Baby” by Edwin Birdsong)…

Timbaland helped Kanye a little with the drums on the track.
