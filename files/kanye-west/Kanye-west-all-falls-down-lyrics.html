About All Falls Down

The lesser-known “Def Poetry Jam” spoken word version of this song ends more abruptly and reverses the order of the first two verses; the result is a masterful meditation on race, class and wealth (Kanye: STICK TO SPOKEN WORD! It suits your voice)

Still, the VO does have some pluses, such as Stacey Dash looking sexy in the video
