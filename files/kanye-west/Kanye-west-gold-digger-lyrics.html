About Gold Digger

Kanye shows his true talent as a rapper by thoroughly examining the “gold digger” phenomenon and shedding light on relationships in a fun and interesting way

This song was a huge hit for good reason. The beat — produced by Kanye with Jon Brion — is big and exciting, the video has showgirls and was directed by Hype Williams, the chorus is catchy, guys love it because they think it ridicules gold diggers, and girls love it because they think it empowers women. And they’re both right — in fact, the song was originally written for Shawnna and performed from a female’s perspective with lines like “I’m not sayin' I’m a gold digger, but I ain’t messin' with no broke n—-as ….”

“Gold Digger” contains samples (as well as an interpolation during the introduction) of “I Got a Woman” by Ray Charles, and a bouncy beat formed from handclaps and scratches by DJ A-Trak.
