About Drive Slow

This track off Kanye’s second album is his homage to the art of creeping through the hood, with a more general message about taking life easy. Slow-flowing rappers GLC and Paul Wall are featured to accentuate the slow tempo.

Kanye sampled Hank Crawford’s “Wildflower” for the beat; the same song was sampled for 2Pac’s “Shorty Wanna Be a Thug” and Eminem’s No One’s Iller
