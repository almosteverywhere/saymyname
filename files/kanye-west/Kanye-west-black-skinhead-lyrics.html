About Black Skinhead

Making its debut on SNL, Black Skinhead is a dark, rebellious and near-tribal record that threads between both Ye’s traditional braggadocio and anti-establishment, anti-racism themes.

Skinhead refers to the cultural movement, which originated in the 60’s in Britain. In the post-war economic boom, with more disposable income available to them, much of the youth became embroiled in fashion trends, music and consumerism.
