About Clique

Clique is the third single off of G.O.O.D. Music’s compilation album, Cruel Summer, following “Mercy” and “New God Flow.” The beat was produced by Hit-Boy (his second credit for a song featuring The Throne), Kanye West, Anthony Kilhoffer and Noah Goldstein.
