About So Appalled

A G.O.O.D. Friday song? With Jay-Z and RZA? And Swizz Beatz doesn’t rap?! We have ourselves a banger!

Pusha T and Cyhi the Prynce — two of the latest signees to Kanye’s label — deliver fiery verses, while Jay-Z is at his absolute best talking down to his critics (Kanye doesn’t sound half-bad, either)

Still, wish we could hear more from RZA…

The song samples “You Are — I Am” by Manfred Mann’s Earth Band.
