About I Love The Dough

Biggie and Jay-Z convincingly refute the popular misconception that money can’t buy happiness

Line by line, the two explain the many ways in which their glamorous lifestyles are superior to those with lesser fortunes. Their excessive riches entitle them to a number of elite perks, including cordon bleu alcohol, classy (or at least hygienic) women, opulent jewelry, front row seats at various sporting events, killing you, and more

So as not to suggest any misogynistic undertones, a woman sings the hook, where she too explicitly expresses her love for money. Balling is an equal opportunity activity
