Rap Genius Dos & Don'ts

Follow these guidelines and the Rap Genius Editors probably won't delete your work.


When annotating lyrics:


• DO say something interesting
• DON'T try to explain the obvious
• DO include links to your references
• DO include images
• DON'T use images with no text
• DON'T annotate individual words
• DO use correct spelling, grammar and punctuation
• DON'T use annotations to correct lyrics
• DON'T be too wordy

When leaving suggestions:


• DON'T spam the site
• DON'T be a jerk
• DO back up your arguments with links
• DO write properly

When adding new songs:


• DON'T add lyrics that are already on the site
• DON'T copy fake songs onto the site
• DO format the lyrics Rap Genius style
• DON'T finish unfinished songs
• DO include a song description and audio link
• DON'T transcribe adlibs

Formatting Help:

  • Before annotating, ask yourself: is this interesting? Does this add anything to the line?
  • Embed a video / Tweet / whatever by pasting the URL

  • Add a link like this:

    [Check out my fave website](http://rapgenius.com)

  • Simple formatting: 

    *This text is in italics*
**This text is bold**
