About Big Pimpin'

An ode to the “pimping” lifestyle: sex with girls without becoming emotionally attached to them

Notable as one of the first songs where Southern rap truly broke into the mainstream with the verses of Bun B and Pimp C, the Texan rap duo known as UGK.

Fine song (in an art-reflecting life kind of way) though this is (who expects a song about pimping to sound like it was made in Cairo?), Jay has expressed embarrassment for and disclaimed the song’s subject in years since:

“I can’t believe I said that, and kept saying it. What kind of animal would say this sort of thing?”
