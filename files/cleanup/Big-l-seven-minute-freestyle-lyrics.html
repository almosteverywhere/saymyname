About Seven-Minute Freestyle

This freestyle was done by Harlem rapper Big L & Brooklyn rapper Jay-Z in 1995 on The Stretch Armstrong & Bobbito Show on WKCR. What’s notable about this Jay’s rapid-fire flow and L’s vicious and gritty lyrics.
