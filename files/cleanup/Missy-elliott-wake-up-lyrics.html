About Wake Up

This Is Not a Test! is the fifth studio album by American rapper Missy Elliott, released by The Goldmind Inc. and Elektra Records on November 25, 2003 in the United States. It was primarily produced by Timbaland, with additional production from Craig Brockman, Nisan Stewart and Elliott herself.

The album received generally favorable reviews from critics and debuted at number thirteen on the Billboard 200, selling 143,600 copies in the first week of release. It has sold 690,000 copies in the United States and has been certified Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).
