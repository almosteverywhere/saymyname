About Blunt Blowin'

“Blunt Blowin” is a song by Lil Wayne and is the second track off his 9th studio album, Tha Carter IV.

Lil Wayne is synonymous with weed at this point, so it makes sense that he builds his second track’s hook around lighting up. It doesn’t contain his most outrageous narcotic exploits (that honor goes to his blatant disregard for his Grammy) but it has his signature slow flow over some eerie violin chords. There’s also a very clever shout-out to Ashton Kutcher!
