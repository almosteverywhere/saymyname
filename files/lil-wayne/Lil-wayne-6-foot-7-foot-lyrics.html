About 6 Foot 7 Foot

The first single off Lil Wayne’s recently released Carter IV album features rap aristocrat Cory Gunz & Lil Wayne yearning to go home (Harry Belafonte style..)

Weezy is actually 5'6", but he rounds up.. (necessary since he’s single) — the 6'7" height (median height of the NBA!) being a play on the lyrics in the sample (“6 foot 7 foot 8 foot bunch!”)

An alternate explanation of the title: In the House of Detention for Men, the oldest jail on Rikers Island, each inmate’s cell is 6 feet by 8 feet
