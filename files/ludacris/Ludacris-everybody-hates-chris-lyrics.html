About Everybody Hates Chris

This song Everybody Hates Chris was put on Luda’s sixth album, Theater of Mind and released on November 22, 2008. It features Chris Rock who is one of the producers of the hit T.V. show Everybody Hates Chris

This song samples Gladys Knight & The Pips It Takes a Whole Lotta Man For a Woman Like Me
Ludacris’s real name is Christopher Brian Bridges. He feels everyone hates him because of all the money, power and women he’s got.
