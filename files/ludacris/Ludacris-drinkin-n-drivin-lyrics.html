About Drinkin' N Drivin'

This song originally leaked on April 2009 and was featured on DJ Smallz & DJ Neptune’s Southern Smoke TV v.1 mixtape.

Rumors circulated that Young Jeezy also had a verse, but that version has yet to be found.
