About Down in tha Dirty

“Down in tha Dirty” is a single from the as yet unreleased compilation album “Strength in Numbers” from the DTP (Disturbing tha Peace) label. It was produced by Clinton Sparks, and samples Bun B’s verse from E-40’s “White Gurl” and the Beastie Boys' “So Wat'cha Want”

The song reached 110 on the US Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs
