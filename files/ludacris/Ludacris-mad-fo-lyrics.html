About Mad Fo

Track #10 off of Luda’s #IDGAF mixtape. This track is actually Luda’s song “Tell Me What They Made For” which original just had Pusha T and Swizz Beatz. This new version features verses from Meek Mill, Chris Brown and Swizz Beatz.
