About Badaboom

Ludacris address the accusations of him “biting” the punchline flow style (sometimes referred to as “pause flow”, “hashtag rap”, or a number of other stupid names) recently popularized by Big Sean and Drake.

This is in response to a claim Drake made in a June 2010 interview with Allhiphop.com.
