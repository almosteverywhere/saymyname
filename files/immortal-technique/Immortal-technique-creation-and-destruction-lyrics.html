About Creation & Destruction

“Creation & Destruction,” the first track off of Revolutionary Vol. 1 by Immortal Technique.

The song was produced by Marley Marl and J-Force.

It samples the song “Long Kiss Goodnight” from the album Life After Death by The Notorious B.I.G.
