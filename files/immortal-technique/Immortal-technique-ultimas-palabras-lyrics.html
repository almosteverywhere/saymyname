About Últimas Palabras

Second-to-last track off Immortal Technique’s 2011 mixtape/album The Martyr.

It’s a speech by Tech about corruption in government, racism, morality, etc. The title is Spanish for “Last Words” which fits since Tech stages his death at the end.
