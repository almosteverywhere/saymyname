About Natural Beauty

In this song, Tech talks about the natural beauty of women, and how the media has a certain way of seeing if a women is beautiful. This leads to self-hatred, and women doing crazy things to make there appearance more desirable according to the medias myths… things like;

  • Skin bleaching
  • Butt implants
  • Boob implants
  • Nose jobs
  • Colloegan implants
  • Relaxing hair

He also goes into how models are way, way to skinny… and if a model is fit, has some muscle, it could be considered ugly.

Another thing he touches is how black features were considered ugly, but now things like full lips and big booties are praised upon.
