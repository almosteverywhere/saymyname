About Freedom

The second single from the re-release of Nicki’s sophomore album, “Pink Friday: Roman Reloaded — The Re-Up”. The new songs on the re-release are said to have more of a “mix-tape” feeling, and Nicki definitely shows that here. The music video was directed by Colin Tilley , and was filmed on November 1 and November 2, 2012 in Dungeness, England. Minaj commented that it is a “meaningful” video. A behind-the-scenes video was released on November 13, 2012, and an official lyric video was released on November 15. The video premiered on 106 and Park on November 19, 2012. The video was uploaded to Nicki Minaj’s official VEVO account shortly after.

Release date: November 2nd, 2012.
