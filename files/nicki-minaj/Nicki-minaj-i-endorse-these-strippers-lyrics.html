About I Endorse These Strippers

“I Endorse These Strippers” is a song by Nicki Minaj from the re-release of Minaj’s second studio album Pink Friday Roman Reloaded: The Re-Up, which features guest vocals from Tyga and Thomas Brinx. It’s the sixth track on the album.
