About I'm Legit

“I’m Legit” is a song by Nicki Minaj from the re-release of Minaj’s second studio album Pink Friday Roman Reloaded: The Re-Up, which features guest vocals from Ciara. The song is the fifth track on the album. The song will be released on November 19, 2012, with the whole album.
