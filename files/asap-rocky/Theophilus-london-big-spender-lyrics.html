About Big Spender

After this video dropped of A$AP Rocky and Theophilus London kicking it and spitting over a nasty instrumental (which Theo had recycled from an older version of the song,) a major buzz circled the internet. During SXSW 2012, Theo and Pretty Flacko performed the song and it was a hit. The track samples “Big Spender” by Peggy Lee.
