About U.O.E.N.O (Extended Remix)

The extended remix to the controversial track including Rick Ross' “date rape” lyrics. Produced by Childish Major, this track brings a lot of talent together including ASVP Rocky, Wiz Khalifa, and Rocko Tha Don. Just rich niggas talking bout how much shit they got and do that we don’t even know bout. On Rocko’s 2013 mixtape Gift of Gab 2.
